2025.08.04 Haina Town Digital Innovation Center, Putuo Shanghai, China

The **Shanghai Mathematics Symposium 2025**, jointly organized by **Feishu** and the **Spanish Institute of Mathematical Sciences (Instituto de Ciencias Matemáticas – ICMAT)**, will be held in Shanghai from **August 4 to 6, 2025**. This event aims to establish a high-level international platform connecting leading mathematicians and interdisciplinary researchers, with a focus on **fundamental research in mathematics**, **mathematics education reform**, and **emerging applications of mathematics across industries**.

See its [website](https://www.feishu.ca/index.php/index/eventsdetail.html?id=299&lang=en).
